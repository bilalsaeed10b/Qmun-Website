Our Journey

QMUN Founded

January 2023

Uzma Saleem establishes QMUN with a vision to create a premier Model UN experience for students across the region.

First Conference

June 2023

QMUN hosts its inaugural conference with 200 delegates from 15 schools, setting a new standard for student-led MUNs.

Team Expansion

September 2023

The core team expands to 12 dedicated members, each bringing unique expertise to enhance the QMUN experience.

International Recognition

February 2024

QMUN receives the "Emerging MUN of the Year" award at the Global Education Summit in Singapore.

Upcoming Conference

October 2025

Our most ambitious event yet, expecting over 500 delegates from 30 countries for a 4-day international conference.
